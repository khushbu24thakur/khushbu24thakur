Venue Information

Welcome to the Khagra Stadium, the iconic venue for the Ultimate Singing Show 2024. Located in the heart of Kishanganj, Bihar, this stadium is known for hosting some of the most prestigious events in the region.

Address: Khagra Stadium, Kishanganj, Bihar (855107)

The Khagra Stadium offers a perfect blend of modern facilities and traditional charm. With a seating capacity of over 10,000, this venue is designed to provide every attendee with a comfortable and immersive experience.

Getting There

The stadium is easily accessible by various modes of transport:

  • By Car: Ample parking space is available for those driving to the venue. Follow the signs for the main entrance, and you'll be directed to the parking areas.
  • By Train: Kishanganj Railway Station is just a 10-minute drive from the stadium, making it convenient for those arriving by train.
  • By Bus: Several local and interstate buses stop near the stadium. The bus terminal is only a 5-minute walk away.
